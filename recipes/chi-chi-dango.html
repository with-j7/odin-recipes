Chi Chi Dango

soft and chewy Japanese treat

The total time needed for this recipe is approximately 1 hour 45 minutes. There are 9 ingredients needed for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 pound glutinous rice flour
  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups water
  • 14 ounce coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon red food color
  • ¼ cup potato starch, or as needed
