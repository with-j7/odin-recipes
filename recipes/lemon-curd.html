Lemon Curd

Homemade lemon curd

Lemon curd can be used as a topping, a filling and it can also be eaten on its own.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest

Steps

  1. Combine lemon juice, sugar, butter, egg yolks, and lemon zest in a saucepan.
  2. Cook over medium-low heat, whisking constantly, until mixture thickens and bubbles, about 5 to 6 minutes.
