Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookie

chewy chocolate chip cookie

The key to perfectly soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies? Brown sugar. It has a higher moisture content than its white counterpart, so it yields softer cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chip

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 165 degrees C. Line with parchment paper.
  2. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together; set aside.
  3. Beat brown sugar, melted butter, and white sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla until light and creamy; add flour mixture and stir until dough is just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto the prepared parchment paper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, about 15 to 17 minutes. Cool on the parchment paper briefly before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
